Description

Reviews

Stila's One-stroke, long-wearing, velvety-matte lipstick with unexpected color impact on application. With its sumptuous, velvety-matte finish, Stay All Day® Matte Lip Color provides high-color release from the very first application, while giving a super-rich, no gravity veil of color. Infused with Cupuaçu Butter- known to help protect and moisturize dry and damaged lips- Stila' s one-stroke lipstick is long-lasting, transfer-proof, mask-proof, and waterproof. Extremely comfortable, buttery, and creamy, this vegan lipstick glides effortlessly and leaves a fabulous, lightweight sensation on the lips!

Ingredients

  • Water– 80.2%
  • C13-16 Isoparaffin – 4.2%
  • Glycerin – 3.0%
  • Diheptyl Succinate – 2.5%
  • Heptyl Undecylenate – 1.8%
  • 1,2-hexanediol – 1.5%
  • Ammonium Acryloyldimethyltaurate/vp Copolymer – 1.5%
  • Polyglyceryl-10 Oleate – 1.5%
  • Propanediol – 1.1%
  • Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil – 1.0%
  • Caprylic/capric Triglyceride – 0.59%
  • Bakuchiol – 0.30%

How to use

Sweep on lips for immediate, impactful color. Close the cap carefully after use.
